Loop Saboteur — glitch / beat-slicer / sabotage engine

This installer will place two plugin formats on your Mac:

  • Audio Unit (.component)  — for Logic, GarageBand, and other AU hosts
  • VST3 (.vst3)             — for Ableton Live, Cubase, Studio One, REAPER

After install, restart your DAW and rescan plugins. Loop Saboteur will
appear under AMFAS in the plugin browser.

This is a private testing build. Please send feedback, bugs, and weird
noises back to Steve. Breakage is expected — that is the whole point.
